Our Philosophy

The Churning That
Produces Wisdom

"मंथन — the ancient act of churning the cosmic ocean — is how amrit, the nectar of wisdom, was wrested from chaos."

Manthanam draws its name and spirit from the Samudra Manthan — the cosmic churning that separated poison from nectar. We believe the same process of rigorous intellectual churning is what India needs today: to distill clear, bold, civilisation-rooted policy from the complex currents of a rapidly changing world.

We are not ideologues. We are strategists. We do not merely criticise — we construct. Every analysis we produce, every discussion we host, every brief we publish is an act of intellectual churning: separating noise from signal, dogma from doctrine, reaction from strategy.

H
Harshdev Garg
Founder & Director, Manthanam

Harshdev Garg is the Founder of Manthanam, a youth-led strategic think tank focused on geopolitics, national security, and civilizational revival. His vision is to build a generation of thinkers who shape India's future through ideas, not imitation.

Vision

To establish Manthanam as India's leading youth-driven strategic think tank — shaping national policy discourse and strengthening Bharat's global position.

Mission

To empower youth with strategic thinking, conduct meaningful discussions, and contribute to India's rise as a global power through research and dialogue.

Our Conviction

"The greatest civilisations did not inherit their greatness — they manufactured it through the ruthless discipline of ideas. Bharat's moment is not coming. It is here."
